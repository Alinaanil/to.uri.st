Privacy policy

If you only read the to.uri.st website, no more information is collected than is typically collected in server logs by web sites in general.

By publishing data on the site, you will be identified by your network IP address. This is a series of four numbers which identifies the Internet address from which you are contacting the site. Depending on your connection, this number may be traceable only to a large Internet service provider, or specifically to your school, place of business, or home. It may be possible that the origin of this IP address could be used in conjunction with any interests you express implicitly or explicitly by editing attractions to identify you even by private individuals.

Logging into the site using your Google account may send some additional traffic and usage data to Google and will set a cookie identifying your browser with regard to your Google account.

Site traffic is monitored by Google Analytics which may set additional cookies.
